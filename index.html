What I Do

I am a co-coordinator at the Moroccan Observatory of SMEs (OMTPME) where is my daily work consists in the construction of an exhaustive and reliable consolidated database on SMEs whose sources come from the Moroccan administrations in charge of this category of companies (mainly BAM, CNSS, DGI, OMPIC) and the realization of regional reports for the Regional Investment Centers (CRI Fez-Meknes, CRI Oriental, CRI Rabat-Salé-Kénitra, CRI Béni Mellal-Khénifra & CRI Casablanca-Settat) and Produce a dashboard of indicators reflecting the demographics, employment, economic and financial health of SMEs. Also i can define the research strategy (data source, analysis models, etc.). Collect, organize, and analyze data. Conducting qualitative and quantitative research. Conduct data analysis with PYTHON. Model, collect, manage, and analyze large databases to extract important information and break down complex problems into digestible chunks and present the results clearly and accurately to technical and non-technical audiences. .
